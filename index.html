Popcorn Attractors

The Popcorn Attractor is a mathematical art technique involving repeatedly plotting then moving a single point around on a graph over and over. It is also known as Pickover Popcorn after Clifford Pickover who is credited with discovering it.
The technique involves taking a starting point then repeatedly moving it slightly and if you repeat this process many times you get a cool picture!

A common approach is creating a 2d histogram that shows the density of points based on where it moved.

Many points are needed, this one is around 2 billion!
